2.b. Movie poster

I got to remake the poster of my favourite movie, so I choose Atlantis.The style is kind off similar to tribal patterns, so I wanted to combine this style with neon glow. I realized for a minimalist poster the patterns are a bit overcrowded, so I changed it to simple line art with a glow effect. My plan was to focus solely on Kida. For me she was the main character because she was powerful and a leader. I learned how to create a realistic glow by using filters in photoshop. I got some feedback and was told it would maybe look better if I only used Kida's zoomed in head instead of a small body. I also liked this idea but in the movie when you see Kida for the first time you have no idea of who she is or if she is important, and i tried to make Kida a little bit anonymous in the poster.
