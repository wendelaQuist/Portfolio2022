Start

I had a few ideas on my mind like for my sprint X like photography or animating. I felt like I could learn a lot if I study more about photography, but I thought it would be boring after a while. When I heard more classmates wanted to animate as well and how much time goes into small projects I decided to keep looking. I have always been interested in crypto currency, because my dad talks about it a lot, but I have never deepened in this topic. I talked to my father and some teachers and came up with the idea to build a crypto trading bot prototype.

Research

At first it was important to understand crypto more. My research was to help me understand how the virtual transactions work and what specifications my bot would need. I looked at other crypto bot companies and came across OneButton trading.com. This project was build by other Fontys students and they use a brilliant system you can't find everywhere. Most crypto bots are free with limitations or you need a subscription even when you lose money. OneButton uses AI bots with different strategies that only take a small part of your profit. So when you don't make any profit it won't take any money. Because this company made the ultimate trading bots I chose to not make a prototype, but focus on my own branding and marketing. I wanted to make a promotion video which excites people to learn about the crypto market just like me.

Branding

Almost all crypto related apps and websites have almost the same color palette. Most of the times it's dark grey with popping colors like blue, pink and orange so I decided to go with the flow because I love these colors and it's more recognizable. Next to the colors, I thought it would look good to give it a retro game vibe. You can paper trade on a lot of platforms, so you won't lose any real money and like a game it can be addictive in a good way. I made a Logo for my brand called: Coin Dealer and some backgrounds in illustrator

Website

I build a website with the top 3 biggest crypto coins values in euros. I made the background in Illustrator and the site shows the current value of the crypto coins via and api connected to Coingecko.com. I decided to only build a home screen only has the home screen but if I had more time I would love to show charts and current news.

Github: CoinDealer site
