Logo Lance Priester

Lance needed a logo to build a brand. Our whole group had to design minimal 5 logos and pitch it for eachother. After we discussed all the logos we chose the best logos of everyone and showed it to Lance.

Progress

When we talked more to Lance it became clear he has a flexible style and wants to appeal to everyone, not just one specific group. Because of Lance's style it became a challenge to balance Lance's thoughts about what is pretty and to be appealing to his target audiance. I received a tip to spend about 10 minutes per logo and then move on to a new design. I used this method to sketch the logos in photoshop and illustrator. To create a strong and recognizable logo I didn't use any colors. We showed the most promising logos to Lance and we used his feedback to create a very personal logo.

Reflection

In my opinion and the one from a design teacher, logo 4 would be best because the shapes are very playful and dynamic. The Idea was to fill some of the grey areas with pictures of nature with a splatter effect which I think represents Lance better.
