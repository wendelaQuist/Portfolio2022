Photoshoot Lance Priester

Tessa and I went to Tilburg together so I could help her with the photography. Lance brought us to the woods where Tessa had taken some beautiful pictures, overall it was a fun and relaxed experience.

Edited pictures in Adobe Photoshop

We talked to Lance about what kind of pictures he would want and said he wanted to have an autumn cozy nature vibe mixed with a kind of trippy style where he takes you away to a different universe just like his music. In some pictures I tried to smooth out his skin and let it have a natural look and in others I tried to create original pictures with intense colours.
