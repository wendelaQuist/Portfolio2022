Cover media exploration for Lance priester

I wanted to design a cover for my group project. I tried to apply the style of our artist Lance Priester and our own style.

Progress

Lance told us he would love to use navy blue in his brand so we found a good color pallete we also used for our own documents. I got a psychedelic vibe from Lance's music and tried to bring it into the cover when designing in illustrator.

❮ ❯

Reflection

Lance told me he liked the line art very much, but he wanted to change some colors. I also got a reaction on the text which can be hard to read, but I know there are album covers who have the same vertical text. I thought it would be better suited to keep te vertical text.
