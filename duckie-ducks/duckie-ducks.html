Duckie ducks

I wanted to create a duck who makes sounds like a real one. It would be better to make the duck quack randomly, but it gets annoying pretty quickly. I decided to create sounds when you click on the duck to make the project less irritating. I made a function which executes when you click on the duck. The play function searches for the mp3 file and plays every time you click on the duck.
